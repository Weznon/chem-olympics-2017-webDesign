  1. Formation-Methenolone is a derivative of dihydrotestosterone (DHT) or more specifically a structurally altered form of DHT. A double bond at carbon one and two is added to the DHT hormone, which in turn greatly increases the hormone’s anabolic nature. It also carries an added 1-methyl group that protects it from being metabolized by the liver.
  2. StructureC22H32O3

  3. Effects- Methenolone’s main effect on the body is that it helps the user retain nitrogen through inhibiting the liver’s functions of converting the nitrogen into what is excreted through urine. Methenolone is also known to help build lean muscle via binding to an androgen receptor. Through a combination of these mechanisms the user will be able to retain muscle easier.

Detection As mentioned earlier methenolone is not metabolized by the liver and thus can be detected using liquid chromatography or gas spectroscopy.
